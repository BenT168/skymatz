Full Description

Jinyun Mountain: Misty Peaks & Ancient Temples. Ride the cable car above the forest canopy and spot the Jialing River winding through the valley below. Walk quiet mountain trails, hear birdsong, and pass the centuries-old Jinyun Temple hidden in the mist. Feeling energetic? Hike 30 minutes to Xianglu Peak for a breathtaking 'above the clouds' view. Tips: Wear comfy hiking shoes. Mornings are best for clean air and great photos!

Chongqing Natural History Museum: A Jurassic Journey. Just a short ride from Jinyun Mountain, explore this sleek, modern museum packed with prehistoric wonders. See full-size dinosaur skeletons, dazzling minerals, and colorful fossils. Interactive exhibits make it fun and educational for all ages — a perfect stop to cool down and dive into natural history.

Southwest University: A Hidden Forest Campus. Often called “the most beautiful university in Chongqing,” this peaceful campus feels more like a park. Walk along tree-lined paths on Tiansheng Road, past ivy-covered walls and quiet lecture halls. Stop by cozy bookshops, student cafés, or grab a local snack at a hidden canteen. A perfect place to slow down, write a postcard, and soak in the serene vibe.

Jingangbei Hot Spring Village: History Meets Relaxation. Step into a 300-year-old riverside village where time seems to slow down. Wander cobbled lanes lined with charming buildings that blend Chinese and Western styles — like walking into 1930s Chongqing. Explore a hidden hot spring village with charming old homes, and watch locals relax and chat in the sun. Quiet, authentic, and full of old-world charm.

Chengjiang Old Street: Finish your journey along the Jialing River at Chengjiang — once a busy ferry port, now a charming old street full of local life. Stroll past wooden houses, street food stalls, and old-time workshops. As the sun sets, sit down with a bowl of local dessert and watch the river flow — a perfect, peaceful farewell to North Chongqing.
