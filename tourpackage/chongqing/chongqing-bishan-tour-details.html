Full Description

Dayuanxiang Woodcarving Museum: Discover a hidden gem known as 'the Dunhuang of Wood.' This private folk museum showcases over 5,000 ancestral wood carvings, 2,000 Buddha statues, and rare Ming-Qing era woodwork. Walk through 8 immersive halls filled with aged timber, traditional family values, and Bayu architectural heritage — a true time capsule of Chongqing’s soul.

Lude Chapel: A Century-Old Blend of East and West. Step into a peaceful century-old chapel with red tiles, white walls, and elegant towers. This historical site blends Western church architecture with Republican-era Chinese details — a unique window into Bishan’s religious and cultural past.

Xiuhu Park & Handicraft Town: As the most iconic wetland park in Bishan District, Xiuhu Park blends urban greenery with natural wetlands in perfect harmony. Stroll along the lakeside paths and take in shimmering waters, graceful waterbirds, and seasonal landscapes. Whether it’s a misty morning or a golden sunset, the park offers a peaceful retreat with scenic charm. Hidden deep within the park is a charming heritage craft village, where you’ll discover hands-on workshops in pottery, tie-dye, woodwork, and bamboo weaving. Visitors can shop for unique handmade creations or join a craft session to experience the warmth and creativity of Chongqing’s living traditions.

Baicaohu Countryside Eco-Zone: Meet tropical vibes in Chongqing. Step into the peaceful countryside and explore a charming mix of local guesthouses, eco-farms, and creative craft shops. Discover the vibrant results of rural revitalization in this serene, artsy village. Stroll through lush paths, snap scenic photos, and pick up unique handmade souvenirs that reflect the spirit of Chongqing’s countryside charm.
