Full Description

The tour begins before the sunset.

Stroll through atmospheric old streets like Huangjiaoya or Xiahaoli, where Chongqing’s layered mountain-city charm comes to life. Your guide will share fascinating stories about the city’s history and transformation while you enjoy a complimentary local snack as the sun begins to set.

As the city lights up, head to Hongyadong, Chongqing’s iconic riverside complex that glows like a fairytale castle at night, and wander through its bustling corridors.

Next, explore Guanyinqiao Bridge, one of Chongqing’s liveliest commercial hubs, illuminated with neon lights and full of local vibes.

The highlight of the evening is a secret viewpoint — known only to locals — where you’ll enjoy a stunning panoramic view of Chongqing’s dramatic skyline, the river winding between skyscrapers, and monorails threading through high-rises. It’s the perfect place to find your favorite angle of this surreal city.

The magical and chill evening ends with a hotel drop-off, leaving you with unforgettable memories of Chongqing’s night glow.
