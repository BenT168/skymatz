Full Description

Leave the bustling city of Chongqing behind and journey deep into the cinematic wilderness of Wulong — a UNESCO World Natural Heritage site and filming location for global blockbusters.

Visit the world-famous karst landscape, lush greenery and streams hide between steep cliffs. Trails wind over water and slopes, cutting through hollowed mountains—a spectacular sight not to miss for those who love wild, rugged hikes.

The magnificent Three Natural Bridges, towering limestone arches set above deep sinkholes and steep cliffs. This surreal terrain served as a filming location for Transformers: Age of Extinction, and also the dramatic cliff scene in Chineses wuxia film, where assassins plunge from the edge — a moment that vividly brings to life an Assassin’s Creed scene.

Refuel and have a brief rest. Continue to venture underground into the magical Furong Cave, wander through a maze of illuminated stalactites, mineral formations, and hidden chambers.

Depending on the season and trail conditions, you may also walk through the Longshuixia Fissure Gorge, a deep canyon carved by time and water, offering peaceful hiking paths between vertical cliffs and cascading waterfalls.

Absorb stories of Wulong’s unique geology, folklore, and cinematic fame from your guide.

This 12-hour private tour includes downtown Chongqing hotel pickup and drop-off, comfortable transportation, entrance tickets, and a pace tailored to your interests.
