Full Description

Catch the breeze at Binhai Road, Dalian’s most scenic coastal drive. Hugging the rugged coastline between the city and the sea, this cliffside promenade is perfect for a morning walk or bike ride. Gaze out at the glittering Yellow Sea, spot hidden coves, and feel the salty wind as you take in Dalian’s natural beauty at every turn.

Step back in time at the Russian Street, where Dalian’s colonial past comes alive. Stroll past rows of restored early 20th-century buildings, once home to Russian traders and diplomats. Today, the cobblestone lane is lined with vintage cafés, souvenir shops, and art galleries that echo the city’s cross-cultural charm.

Marvel at the stunning Xinghai Square — one of Asia’s largest city squares. With its grand fountains, wide open spaces, and sweeping sea views, it’s the heart of modern Dalian. From here, you’ll catch sight of the iconic Xinghai Bay Bridge stretching across the water like a silk ribbon, a symbol of the city’s ambition and grace.

Wander through Fisherman’s Wharf, a seaside gem where old-world harbor life meets modern design. Enjoy oceanfront dining, admire charming European-style buildings, and watch sailboats bob gently in the marina. It’s the perfect spot to slow down and breathe in the coastal lifestyle.

Explore Dalian Forest Zoo, home to both lush natural landscapes and rare animals like giant pandas and Siberian tigers. A scenic cable car ride connects the northern and southern sections of the park — and as the day winds down, find a quiet spot to enjoy a golden seaside sunset over the hills, ending your journey with calm and wonder.
