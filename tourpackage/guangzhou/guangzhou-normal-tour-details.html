Full Description

Start your journey at the beautifully preserved Chen Clan Ancestral Hall, where your guide will walk you through the rich cultural heritage and intricate craftsmanship of Lingnan architecture.

Then dive into local life at a traditional food market tucked away in a nearby neighborhood. Your guide will help you communicate with local vendors and introduce you to authentic Cantonese street snacks in a residential setting rarely visited by tourists.

Next, explore the peaceful charm of Shamian Island, where European-style buildings, leafy promenades, and river views recall the city’s colonial past.

Visit the former residence of Bruce Lee, a hidden highlight for martial arts fans and pop culture enthusiasts. Learn about his Cantonese roots and early life in Guangzhou.

As the sky darkens, stroll through the revitalized Yongqing Fang, where historic alleyways meet modern art, design shops, and street food stalls — a lively mix of old and new Canton.

Continue to the Grand Buddha Temple (Dafo Temple), one of the city’s oldest Buddhist temples. In the warm glow of incense and lanterns, take in the peaceful energy of this historic site and explore the bustling streets nearby.

End your tour at a hidden viewpoint with a stunning panorama of the Canton Tower and Pearl River nightscape — the perfect way to wrap up an immersive afternoon and evening in Guangzhou.
