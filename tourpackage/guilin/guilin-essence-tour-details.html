Full Description

Chuanshan Scenic Area: Begin your day with a 30-minute walk to the hilltop of Chuanshan Park. Enjoy sweeping views of Guilin’s limestone peaks and the Li River winding through the city. Along the way, explore Moon Cave — a natural karst cave with fascinating rock formations.

Osmanthus Handicraft Workshop: Visit a cozy courtyard studio hidden in a back alley and handcraft your own fragrant sachet using dried osmanthus flowers, mugwort, and mint. Learn the cultural meanings behind each herb, and take home a personal souvenir tied to Chinese seasonal wellness.

Elephant Trunk Hill: Capture a classic photo at the best viewpoint of Elephant Trunk Hill — the city’s symbolic landmark shaped like a giant elephant drinking from the river. No need to enter the park; the guide will show you where to get the perfect shot.

Dongxi Lane, Zhengyang Pedestrian Street & Xiaoyao Tower: Stroll through the historic Dongxi Lane, filled with Ming and Qing architecture, indie boutiques, and art cafés. Visit the Xiaoyao Tower ruins and listen to stories of ancient merchants and poets. Then explore the lively pedestrian street for snacks and souvenirs.

Old South Gate & Rongshan Lakes: Walk past the stone remains of Guilin’s ancient south gate and enjoy a lakeside stroll along Rong and Shan Lakes. This tranquil area features pagodas, bridges, and water reflections that create postcard-perfect views.

Two Rivers Four Lakes Cruise: End your day with a relaxing boat cruise along Guilin’s inner waterways — the Li River, Taohua River, and four connecting lakes. Glide past pagodas, stone bridges, tree-lined banks, and locals fishing or dancing. The perfect way to soak in Guilin’s charm from the water.
