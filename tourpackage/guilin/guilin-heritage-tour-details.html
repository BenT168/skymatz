Full Description

Start your cultural journey with a comfortable ride out of the city, passing through serene rural landscapes.

Daxu Ancient Town: Wander through stone-paved alleys of this 1,000-year-old riverside town. Admire wooden houses with intricate carvings and explore a mix of Lingnan and traditional Guangxi architecture. A perfect place to snap vintage-style photos and soak in old-world charm.

Dongli Ancient Village & Intangible Heritage Workshops: Step into a quiet mountain village where time slows down. Here, you can choose to join hands-on heritage workshops such as blue-dye fabric printing, hand-thrown pottery, wood carving, or bamboo weaving. A local farm-style lunch is included—fresh, hearty, and rooted in tradition.

Dashiwan Scenic Spot: Head to a hilltop viewpoint known as 'Guilin's Riverside Scroll.' Overlook lush hills and winding rivers that stretch like ink-wash paintings. It's a quiet and less-touristy spot, ideal for nature lovers and photographers.

Huangbu Reflection – 20 Yuan Banknote View: End the day at one of Guilin’s most iconic views—the real-life scene from China’s 20 yuan note. Watch karst peaks mirrored in the Li River and take that must-have photo.
