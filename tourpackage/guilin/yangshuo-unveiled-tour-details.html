Full Description

Start your adventure in the picturesque Ten-Mile Gallery near Moon Hill. Glide down a bamboo raft through emerald peaks, charming villages, and cultural gems like Longtan Village, capturing the poetic beauty of Yangshuo.

Experience the serenity of the Yulong River, known for its jade-green waters, gentle currents, and stunning landscapes. Float past bamboo groves, verdant hills, and tranquil countryside while soaking in the peaceful vibes.

Marvel at the 500-year-old Fuli Bridge, an ancient masterpiece with a "full moon" reflection. This historic spot offers panoramic views of Yangshuo’s idyllic countryside, where nature and history blend seamlessly.

Continue your journey in the serene fields of Wulong Spring, where scattered peaks, lush farmlands, and poetic scenery create a tranquil escape.

Wander through the timeless streets of Xingping Old Town, a 1,700-year-old town filled with ancient buildings, cobblestone paths. A little secret: you will taste two local specialties and a drink in Xingping Old Town, making this stroll more relaxing and pleasant. Witness the stunning karst peaks and shimmering Li River landscapes immortalized on the 20-yuan note. This is Yangshuo’s most iconic view—perfect for a photo memory.
