Highlights

  • Taste juicy seafood soup dumplings made fresh to order
  • Try crispy shrimp-stuffed pancakes with egg and chives — a bold coastal snack
  • Stroll through Pichaiyuan’s buzzing alleys full of flavors, stories, and charm
  • Cool off with rich, creamy gelato from a 30-year-old neighborhood favorite
  • Select your sizzling BBQ skewers at a local street joint loved by generations

Full Description

Soup Bao Bun: Savor a steaming basket of "Quan Jia Fu" (all-inclusive) soup Bao Buns, generously stuffed with local seafood and wrapped in delicate skin — made fresh to order, just like locals love them.

Shrimp pancakes: Dive into bold flavors with shrimp “ha bing” pancakes — crispy outside, juicy inside — served with a rich, savory garlic chives and eggs.

Guided Walk - Pichaiyuan Alleyways: Step back in time in this vibrant labyrinth of old Qingdao life. Learn how locals lived, traded, and ate across generations. Discover hidden murals and nostalgic shop signs.

Kaixue Gelato: Cool down with a scoop of Qingdao’s beloved local ice cream brand — soft, flavorful, and full of hometown charm.

Old-style Bakery: Taste time-tested flavors with freshly baked glutinous rice bread — choose from red bean, purple rice, millet, and more. A bite of old Qingdao you can’t find elsewhere.

Sister Wang BBQ: End your food tasting with three skewers of your choice — spicy beef, squid, tofu skin and more — grilled to perfection with Qingdao’s signature barbecue seasoning.

Guided Walk - St. Michael’s Cathedral (Exterior): Snap a photo at the iconic Gothic cathedral and learn about its significance in Qingdao’s colonial history.

Guided Walk - Guanxiangshan Street + Sunset Drink. Walk past hillside European houses and quaint lanes as you sip a refreshing fruit tea or yogurt drink. This peaceful path leads us to our final goodbye point.
