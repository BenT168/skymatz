Full Description

Start your journey at the Shanghai Urban History Exhibition Hall inside the Oriental Pearl Tower. Explore Shanghai’s transformation through immersive exhibits and historic artifacts.

Stroll down Shanghai’s vibrant food street, lined with local delicacies and colorful neon signs, creating a lively culinary experience. Enjoy an authentic taste of Shanghai with steamed pork soup dumplings and fish ball soup.

Walk along Wukang Road, famous for its lush trees, European-style villas, and 37 historic buildings. Don’t miss the iconic Wukang Mansion, a striking wedge-shaped architectural gem.

Discover the artsy vibe of Tianzifang, where Shikumen alleyways are transformed into boutique shops, galleries, and cafés, blending tradition and creativity.

Wander through Yu Garden, a serene masterpiece of classical Chinese design, featuring elegant pavilions, rockeries, and lush greenery. Treat yourself to Shanghai’s famous street food at the bustling Yu Garden bazaar, filled with savory and sweet local delights.

End your day with breathtaking views of The Bund’s historic buildings and Lujiazui’s illuminated skyline, a perfect finale to your Shanghai adventure.
