Full Description

Sea-View Metro: Start your day with one of the most scenic rides on Xiamen’s Metro Line 1. As the train glides over the elevated sea bridge, you'll see endless tidal flats, flocks of egrets, and panoramic views of the ocean. This isn’t just a commute — it’s a moving postcard introducing you to Jimei's unique coastal charm.

Dragon Boat Pond & Nanyang-style Architecture: Enter the serene surroundings of the Dragon Boat Pond. Lining its banks are grand red-brick buildings with flying eaves — fine examples of the “Jiageng Style” architecture created by philanthropist Chen Jiageng. Daonan Tower and Nanxun Building reflect a perfect blend of Minnan tradition and Southeast Asian elegance. Continue to the Opera Square, still a lively community venue for festivals and traditional performances.

Jiageng Culture & Spirit: Head to Jiageng Park for a short stroll under the shade of banyan trees. Then step into Ao Garden — a peaceful sanctuary filled with intricate stone carvings, pagodas, and memorial arches that reflect the spirit of its namesake. Finish this segment at the Chen Jiageng Memorial Hall, where photos, artifacts, and immersive exhibits tell the life story of one of China’s most influential overseas philanthropists.

Shigu Road Food Street: Walk over to Shigu Road, a narrow but bustling street filled with sizzling woks, seafood steamers, and cheerful vendors. Try classic local dishes such as satay noodles with rich broth, crispy oyster omelettes, peanut soup, or fried spring rolls. It’s an authentic Xiamen food experience — no frills, just flavor.

Xiamen Garden Expo Park & Moon Plaza: Visit the Garden Expo Park, a sprawling open-air display of China’s regional landscaping traditions. Wander from Suzhou-style courtyards to Yunnan-inspired plantings, with bridges, streams, and pavilions in between. Close by is the minimalist Moon Plaza, where the ocean horizon creates the perfect photo opportunity.

Ten-Mile Dyke Sunset Walk: As the sun begins to set, take a relaxed walk along the Ten-Mile Dyke — one of Xiamen’s most scenic coastal paths. This seawall path offers views of the city skyline on one side and the open ocean on the other. With the sky turning gold and sea breeze on your face, this is the perfect way to unwind and reflect on a full day of discovery.
