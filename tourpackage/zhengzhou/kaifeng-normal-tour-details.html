Full Description

Step into a modern, well-curated Kaifeng Museum that showcases Kaifeng’s historical evolution. Highlights include: Model of the Song Dynasty capital — an aerial look at how the ancient city was structured; Canal-trade artifacts — relics from shipwrecks that speak of Kaifeng’s maritime legacy; Traditional opera & local arts exhibit — engaging multimedia displays bring the region’s intangible heritage to life.

Hands-On Heritage: Incense Craft Workshop (Solid Perfume or Incense Seal). Join a peaceful, sensory-rich workshop to learn one kind of traditional Chinese fragrance techniques: Solid Perfume (Xiang Gao): Blend natural essential oils with beeswax to create a pocket-sized scent balm. Incense Seal (Xiang Zhuan): Grind aromatic herbs and press them into ancient-shaped molds, then light them to enjoy a slow-burning ritual used by scholars for centuries. You’ll take home your handmade item as a unique travel keepsake.

Qingming Riverside Landscape Garden. Experience a full-scale recreation of life in a Song Dynasty city based on the famous painting "Along the River During the Qingming Festival." You can expect live performances: From street acrobats to Song weddings and judge Bao’s processions; Entertainment facilities: Song-style pirate ship, carousel, roller coaster; Craft alleys: try dough sculpture, calligraphy, or just enjoy the photo-worthy setting.

City Walk of the Kaifeng Old Town. Discover Kaifeng beyond tourist routes through a guided walk in the old city: Shanshan-Gan Guild Hall (Exterior): Admire its wood carvings and colorful rooflines—a testament to the merchant glory of Ming and Qing dynasties; Kaifeng Fu Square & Bao Gong Shrine (Exterior view): Learn about the famed judge’s legacy of justice and honesty; Local alleys near Xi Si Market & Daxiangguo Temple: Stroll through teahouses, antique stalls, herbal shops, and puppet stages for a glimpse of real daily life.
